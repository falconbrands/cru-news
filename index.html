A message from CRU Cannabis

In response to the recent concerns regarding vape-related health illness, we would like to provide you with some important information concerning our products.

While no specific device or substance has been linked tothe illnesses, some research has indicated that extremelyhigh levels of the chemical vitamin E acetate were found in cannabis-containing vaping products that were analyzed as part of the investigation.

Please be assured the CRU Cannabis does not use any vitamin E acetate in any of its vape products or production thereof.

We are committed to the quality and safety of our products. As a company, we are concerned with the development of these health issues and will continue to closely monitor the status of the research and findings, in relation to our production. The safety of our consumers is very important to us.

There have not been any incidents we are aware of being linked to any CRU Cannabis vape products.CRU Cannabis products are made with only the highest quality distillates and terpenes. They are tested by state licensed labs.

Thank you for your continuedlove and support.
